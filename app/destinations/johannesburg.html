Destination Johannesburg

Johannesburg, South Africa is home to the Red Hat South Africa office, located at 138 West Street, Sandown, Sandton, 2196 — in the Sandton central business district, the financial capital of Africa and one of the continent's most dynamic and commercially important addresses. Sandton is home to the Johannesburg Stock Exchange, the African operations of most major multinational corporations, and a growing technology ecosystem that is driving digital transformation across Sub-Saharan Africa. Red Hat's Johannesburg office serves not only the South African enterprise market but anchors the company's broader African technology strategy.

Steps from 138 West Street in Sandton, The Saxon Boutique Hotel's restaurant is one of Johannesburg's most celebrated fine dining destinations — a magnificent estate property with a kitchen earning consistent top marks for its refined modern South African cuisine featuring extraordinary local ingredients: Karoo lamb, Kalahari truffles, Cape seafood, and exceptional South African wines from the Stellenbosch and Franschhoek regions. For a more characterful local experience, the Sandton restaurants of Melrose Arch — particularly Marble and Tashas — consistently rank among Johannesburg's finest and most beloved dining destinations for the city's tech community.

Visiting the Red Hat Johannesburg office means arriving in the economic engine of the African continent — a city of extraordinary energy, resilience, and ambition, and one that is undergoing a renaissance in its creative and tech sectors centered on areas like Maboneng, Braamfontein, and the 44 Stanley district of Milpark. The Apartheid Museum is one of the most powerful and important museum experiences in the world and is essential for understanding modern South Africa. The Cradle of Humankind World Heritage Site nearby is where some of the earliest human fossils were discovered — giving Johannesburg a uniquely profound perspective on the long arc of human progress, of which open source software is clearly a part.
