Destination Bengaluru

Bengaluru (Bangalore), Karnataka, India is home to another Dynatrace India office, located at 1st Floor, Table Space Towers, 4th Cross Road, Kaveri Nagar, Krishnarajapuram, Bengaluru, Karnataka, 560048 — in the Whitefield-Krishnarajapuram corridor, one of Bengaluru's major technology and IT zones. Bengaluru is India's Silicon Valley — the country's technology capital, home to hundreds of global technology companies, a vast pool of exceptional engineering talent, and a climate that has earned it the nickname "Garden City" for its year-round mild weather at 900 meters above sea level. India's tech boom runs through Bengaluru, and Dynatrace is very much part of that story.

In the greater Bengaluru area, Karavalli at the Taj Gateway Hotel on Residency Road is consistently celebrated as one of India's finest coastal cuisine restaurants, renowned for its exceptional Mangalorean, Coorg, and Kerala dishes — coconut-rich curries, fresh catch from the Malabar and Konkan coasts, appam with stew, and a warm, heritage atmosphere that has made it a Bengaluru institution for decades. The seafood thalis are considered among the best in South India, and the ambiance of the traditional carved wooden interior is deeply memorable.

Visiting the Dynatrace Bengaluru office means arriving in a city where the IPL cricket matches at M. Chinnaswamy Stadium are a local religion, the microbrewery scene rivals any city in Asia for craft beer innovation, and the startup ecosystem at Koramangala and Indiranagar operates at an intensity that makes Silicon Valley look relaxed by comparison. The Bengaluru team is deeply technical, passionate about engineering excellence, and proud of operating from the capital of India's software industry — the city that built much of the digital world that Dynatrace now helps observe and protect.
