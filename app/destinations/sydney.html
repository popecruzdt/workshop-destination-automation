Destination Sydney

Sydney, Australia is home to the Dynatrace Asia Pacific office, located at Level 18, 100 Miller Street, North Sydney, NSW 2060. Perched above the vibrant North Sydney business district with sweeping views of Sydney Harbour and the iconic Harbour Bridge, this office is proof that sometimes the best perk at work is simply looking out the window. Sydney is Australia's largest city and one of the most livable and dynamic cities in the world.

A short walk from the office, Aqua Dining in Milsons Point is one of Sydney's most celebrated waterfront restaurants, consistently earning top marks for its contemporary Australian cuisine, locally sourced seafood, and unbeatable views of the Harbour Bridge and Opera House. Whether you're celebrating a successful deployment or just a good standup, the snapper ceviche and handpicked regional wine list make it an occasion to remember.

Visiting the Dynatrace Sydney office means starting your day with a harbour view that absolutely no dashboard can replicate — though Davis AI will certainly try. Sydney offers world-class surf beaches, the Blue Mountains just an hour away, and a coffee culture so serious that "flat white" was practically invented here. Fair warning: after experiencing a Sydney sunrise over the harbour from the 18th floor, remote work will never quite feel the same again.
