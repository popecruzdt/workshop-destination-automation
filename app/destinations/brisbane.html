Destination Brisbane

Brisbane, Queensland, Australia is home to the Dynatrace Australia Queensland office, located at 240 Queen Street, Christie Conference Centre, Brisbane, QLD 4000 — in the heart of Brisbane's CBD on its main commercial boulevard. Brisbane is Australia's fastest-growing major city, a subtropical powerhouse of enterprise technology and a city transformed by its hosting of the 2032 Olympic Games. The Brisbane River curves dramatically through the city, South Bank's cultural precinct sits across the water, and the Dynatrace team here serves one of Australia's most rapidly digitizing enterprise markets.

Steps from the office on Queen Street and throughout Eagle Street Pier, Stokehouse Q earns consistent top marks as one of Brisbane's finest dining destinations, celebrated for its modern Australian cuisine, outstanding Queensland produce (including exceptional Moreton Bay bugs and local barramundi), and a riverside terrace that captures the easy glamour of subtropical Brisbane at its best. The Queensland mud crab and locally farmed beef selections are consistently outstanding and represent some of the finest produce available anywhere in Australia.

Visiting the Dynatrace Brisbane office means arriving in a city that trades on warmth — both climatic and human. The Story Bridge adventure climb, the South Bank Parklands with its lagoon-style swimming complex, and the day-trip accessibility of the Gold Coast beaches and the Sunshine Coast hinterland make Brisbane a destination with remarkable variety within arm's reach. The Queensland team carries the city's relaxed, can-do spirit into everything they do, which makes them both excellent collaborators and very good company at Friday afternoon drinks on the riverside.
