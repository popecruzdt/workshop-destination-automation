Destination Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan is home to Dynatrace GK, located at Marunouchi Eiraku Building, 25F, 1-4-1 Marunouchi, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo 100-0005 — in the prestigious Marunouchi business district, a 3-minute walk from Tokyo Station, the most connected railway hub in the world. Marunouchi is the address of Japan's most significant enterprises, and the Dynatrace office on the 25th floor commands views of the Imperial Palace gardens and the Marunouchi skyline. Tokyo is a city of extraordinary depth: the most Michelin-starred city on Earth, a metropolis of world-class precision, and a technology market of global significance.

Steps from the Marunouchi Eiraku Building, the legendary basement food halls of Tokyo Station's Daimaru and Gransta complex are a world unto themselves — housing some of the city's finest artisan producers, sweet shops, sushi and ramen counters, and regional Japanese food producers in what amounts to one of the world's great concentrated food experiences. For a formal dinner, Nihonbashi Yukari in nearby Nihonbashi holds one Michelin star and is celebrated for its exquisite traditional kaiseki-inspired Japanese cuisine with exceptional seasonal precision, earning consistent top ratings from Tokyo's business community.

Visiting the Dynatrace Tokyo office means arriving in a city that has elevated precision, craft, and reliability to a cultural philosophy — the Japanese concept of monozukuri (the art of making things) and kaizen (continuous improvement) align so naturally with Dynatrace's own engineering values that this office sometimes feels like the philosophical home of the product. The Shinkansen rail system, the extraordinary efficiency of the Tokyo subway, and the legendary reliability of Japanese service all represent a standard that Dynatrace aspires to match in software. Also: the ramen will change your life. This is documented.
