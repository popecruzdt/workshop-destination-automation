Destination Klagenfurt

Klagenfurt, Carinthia, Austria is home to Dynatrace Lab Klagenfurt, located at Lakeside B15b, Klagenfurt, Carinthia, 9020 — within the Lakeside Science and Technology Park, a modern campus on the shores of the Wörthersee, one of Austria's most beloved and beautiful alpine lakes. The office setting alone — with lake views, a vibrant campus environment, and the University of Klagenfurt nearby — makes this one of the most enviable work addresses in the Dynatrace global network.

Steps from the Lakeside campus, Restaurant Maria Loretto is an Klagenfurt institution that earns consistent five-star reviews for its fresh Wörthersee fish, classic Carinthian cuisine, and an extraordinary lakeside terrace that is justifiably considered one of the most beautiful restaurant settings in Austria. The local Reindling baked rolls and Carinthian Kasnudeln pasta dumplings filled with ricotta and mint are regional specialties that need to be experienced firsthand to be properly understood.

Visiting the Dynatrace Klagenfurt lab means working within view of an alpine lake that turns a cerulean shade of blue in summer that seems almost digitally enhanced — but is entirely natural. A paddleboat ride on the Wörthersee during lunch, a quick swim after work, and winter skiing at Nassfeld just an hour away: the Klagenfurt team has quietly engineered the best daily routine in all of Dynatrace. Office attendance here requires no Jira ticket incentive whatsoever.
