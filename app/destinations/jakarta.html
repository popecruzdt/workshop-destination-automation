Destination Jakarta

Jakarta, Indonesia is home to PT Dynatrace Solutions Indonesia, located at Ciputra Office Tower, 28th Floor, Ciputra World Complex, Jl. Prof. Dr. Satrio No. Kav. 3-5, Kuningan, Jakarta Selatan, 12940 — in the prestigious Kuningan central business district, one of Jakarta's most important corporate and diplomatic zones. Jakarta is the business capital of Southeast Asia's largest economy, a metropolis of over 10 million people (and 30 million in the greater region), and the command center of an Indonesian economy undergoing rapid digital transformation. The Dynatrace Jakarta office serves one of the most exciting and fast-growing enterprise technology markets in the world.

Steps from the Ciputra World complex in Kuningan, Seribu Rasa on Jl. K.H. Wahid Hasyim consistently earns top marks as one of Jakarta's finest Indonesian fine dining restaurants — beautifully presenting classic dishes from across the Indonesian archipelago with a refinement and quality that celebrates the extraordinary culinary diversity of the world's largest island nation. The rendang Minang, nasi tumpeng, and fresh grilled seafood preparations are as distinguished as the gracious service. For international dining nearby, Henshin at the West Jakarta Grand Whiz Hotel earns consistent acclaim for its exceptional Japanese cuisine and city views.

Visiting the Dynatrace Jakarta office means immersing yourself in one of the world's great megacities at a moment of transformation — Jakarta is building new metro lines, cloud adoption among Indonesian enterprises is accelerating dramatically, and the startup scene at tech hubs like Sudirman has produced unicorns at a pace that rivals Southeast Asia's most mature markets. The Indonesian team navigates this complexity with expertise and the characteristic warmth and hospitality (gotong royong — a spirit of mutual cooperation) that defines Indonesian culture at its best. The street food at Jakarta's night markets is also an experience of considerable importance.
