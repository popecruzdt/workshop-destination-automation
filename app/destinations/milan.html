Destination Milan

Milan, Italy is home to the Dynatrace Italy office, located at Viale E. Forlanini, 23, Milan, 20134 — in the eastern district of Italy's business and fashion capital, close to Linate Airport and well-connected to the city center. Milan is the economic engine of Italy, a global hub for finance, design, fashion, and increasingly, enterprise technology. The Dynatrace Milan team serves one of Europe's most sophisticated and digitally ambitious enterprise markets from a city that approaches everything — including cloud infrastructure — with an eye for both performance and elegance.

A short Metro or tram ride into central Milan, Il Luogo di Aimo e Nadia holds two Michelin stars and is widely regarded as one of Italy's finest restaurants — a temple of refined Milanese and Italian regional cuisine where extraordinary seasonal ingredients are transformed into dishes of rare sophistication and depth. For something closer to the office, Ristorante Esco Bistro Mediterraneo on Viale Corsica earns outstanding reviews from Milan's tech and business community for its modern Mediterranean menu, excellent Lombard wines, and a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere.

Visiting the Dynatrace Milan office means coming to a city where the Duomo cathedral, Leonardo da Vinci's Last Supper, the La Scala opera house, and the most concentrated fashion district in the world are all within a tramline of each other — a city that has been setting the global standard for what "excellence" looks like for over five centuries. The Milan team brings this same sensibility to its work: things should function flawlessly, and they should also look great while doing it. An entirely reasonable position for a Dynatrace team, and an entirely Italian one.
