Destination Canberra

Canberra, Australian Capital Territory, Australia is home to the Dynatrace Australia federal government office, located at Level 6, 490 Northbourne Avenue, Dickson, ACT 2602 — in the Dickson commercial precinct, just north of Canberra's city center and well connected to the parliamentary and government precincts that define Australia's capital. Canberra is Australia's purpose-built capital, designed from scratch in the early 20th century, and today the seat of federal government, home to Australia's defense and intelligence agencies, and a major hub for government digital transformation programs where Dynatrace plays an important role.

In the Dickson suburb close to the office, the Asian Food Quarter on Woolley Street is one of Canberra's most beloved and vibrant dining precincts — a compact, multicultural stretch of exceptional Vietnamese, Korean, Chinese, and Japanese restaurants that has become a Canberra institution. For a more formal dining experience, Aubergine in Griffith is consistently rated Canberra's finest restaurant, earning top national reviews for its impeccable modern Australian tasting menu and extraordinary focus on local and regional produce from the surrounding Tablelands.

Visiting the Dynatrace Canberra office means exploring a city that surprises visitors who expect it to be purely administrative. The Australian War Memorial is one of the most moving museum experiences in the country, the National Gallery of Australia holds exceptional collections including major Aboriginal and international art, and the surrounding Snowy Mountains region offers some of Australia's best skiing in winter and spectacular hiking in summer. The Canberra team works at the intersection of cloud technology and government digital sovereignty — making it one of the most strategically interesting offices in the Dynatrace network.
