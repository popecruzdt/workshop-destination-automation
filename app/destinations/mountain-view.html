Destination Mountain View

Mountain View, California is home to the Dynatrace West Coast office, located at 401 Castro Street, Second Floor, Mountain View, CA 94041, right on the main street of one of Silicon Valley's most walkable and beloved tech towns. Nestled between the Google campus to the north and the Santa Cruz Mountains to the south, Mountain View is where the future of tech is quietly assembled — one commit at a time.

A short stroll from the office, Chez TJ is a legendary Mountain View institution — a nationally celebrated fine dining destination housed in a historic Victorian that has been earning Michelin recognition for its seasonally inspired California-French cuisine since 1982. Equal parts elegant and warm, it's the kind of place where a celebratory dinner for a successful release feels perfectly at home alongside a bottle of Napa Cab.

Visiting the Dynatrace Mountain View office puts you squarely in the epicenter of the global technology industry, within walking distance of the biggest names in software and hardware. The office is a quick bike ride from the stunning shoreline of the San Francisco Bay Trail, the weather is genuinely sunny almost every single day of the year, and the Castro Street farmers market will make you question every grocery store decision you've ever made. Pro tip: bring sunscreen and a sense of wonder.
