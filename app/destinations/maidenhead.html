Destination Maidenhead

Maidenhead, Berkshire, United Kingdom is home to the Dynatrace UK office, located at Quantum, 60 Norden Road, Maidenhead, Berkshire, SL6 4AY — a modern business campus in one of the Thames Valley's most established enterprise technology corridors. Maidenhead sits 35 minutes from London Paddington by Elizabeth line train, and is surrounded by a tech ecosystem that includes the European headquarters of many global technology companies. The Thames runs just south of the town, Windsor Castle is a short drive away, and Bray — arguably England's greatest culinary village — is just minutes from the office.

Remarkably, the world's most Michelin-starred restaurant per square kilometer is within a short drive of the Dynatrace Maidenhead office: Bray hosts both Heston Blumenthal's The Fat Duck (three Michelin stars, one of the world's most inventive dining experiences) and Alain Roux's The Waterside Inn (three Michelin stars, a temple of classic French cuisine on the Thames). Having two three-Michelin-starred restaurants within a few minutes of your office is not something most Dynatrace locations can claim, and the Maidenhead team is rightly proud of this highly irrelevant but thoroughly enjoyable geographical advantage.

Visiting the Dynatrace Maidenhead office means stepping into the heart of the Thames Valley technology corridor — one of Europe's most established and productive enterprise tech ecosystems. Windsor Great Park is a short drive for lunch walks, Henley-on-Thames and its famous Royal Regatta are just up the river, and London's full cultural spectacle is a quick train ride away. The UK team is known for its dry wit, technical depth, and an unshakeable conviction that tea solves most problems — a hypothesis that has not yet been disproven by any amount of distributed tracing data.
