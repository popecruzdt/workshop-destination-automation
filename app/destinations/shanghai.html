Destination Shanghai

Shanghai, China is home to the Dynatrace China East office, located at Unit 18G, Zhao Feng World Trade Building, No. 369 Jiangsu Road, Chang Ning District, Shanghai, 200050 — in the upscale Chang Ning district, a major commercial hub well connected to Shanghai's Jing'an and Hongqiao business centers. Shanghai is China's financial capital and the country's most cosmopolitan city — a place where the futuristic Pudong skyline of Lujiazui faces the preserved colonial grandeur of the Bund across the Huangpu River, embodying the city's extraordinary capacity to hold tradition and innovation simultaneously.

Near the Zhao Feng building and throughout Chang Ning, Ultraviolet by Paul Pairet is one of the world's most unique dining experiences — a 10-seat restaurant where each of 20 courses is accompanied by a synchronized multi-sensory production of light, sound, scent, and tablescape, earning consistent rankings among the world's top restaurants. For a more accessible but equally exceptional local experience, Jesse Restaurant on Tianping Road has been celebrated for decades as Shanghai's finest Shanghainese home-style cuisine — the braised pork belly (hong shao rou) and drunken chicken are essential orders.

Visiting the Dynatrace Shanghai office means arriving in one of the most stimulating, fast-moving, and visually extraordinary cities on Earth — a city where the Maglev train connects you to the airport at 430 km/h, where the French Concession's plane tree-lined boulevards feel like a time capsule, and where the scale and pace of digital transformation in China's enterprise sector is simply breathtaking to witness close-up. The Shanghai team operates at the center of this transformation with deep expertise and an energy that matches the city's own.
