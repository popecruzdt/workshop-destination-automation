Destination Sao Paulo

São Paulo, Brazil is home to Dynatrace Software do Brasil Ltda., located at Av. das Nações Unidas, 14.261, Ala B, 26° andar, Sala 136, Vila Gertrudes, São Paulo, CEP 04794-000 — in the southern Marginal Pinheiros corridor, São Paulo's most prestigious and rapidly growing corporate district, home to Brazil's major financial institutions, multinationals, and technology companies along the banks of the Pinheiros River. São Paulo is the largest city in South America, the economic engine of Brazil, and a city whose tech sector is growing at a pace that makes it one of the most exciting enterprise software markets in the developing world.

Steps from the Nações Unidas avenue office in the Vila Olímpia and Itaim Bibi dining districts, Tuju holds one Michelin star and is consistently celebrated as one of Brazil's finest restaurants — Chef Ivan Ralston's seasonal tasting menu celebrates Brazilian biodiversity with extraordinary creativity, sourcing directly from small producers and indigenous communities across Brazil's extraordinary range of ecosystems: Cerrado, Amazon, Pantanal, and Atlantic Forest all make appearances on the plate. For the iconic São Paulo dining experience, D.O.M. by Alex Atala nearby holds two Michelin stars and has introduced the world to the incomparable depth of Amazonian and Brazilian ingredients at the highest level of culinary art.

Visiting the Dynatrace São Paulo office means arriving in a city that never, under any interpretation, slows down. São Paulo has more Japanese descendants outside Japan than any other city in the world, the largest Arab community in the Americas, and a cultural mosaic that expresses itself through one of the world's great restaurant cities and a street art scene (particularly in the Villa Madalena neighborhood) that is internationally acclaimed. The Brazilian team brings the country's famous warmth, creativity, and resilience to a market that tests all three in equal and considerable measure. Vida boa!
