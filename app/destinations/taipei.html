Destination Taipei

Taipei, Taiwan is home to the Red Hat Taiwan office, located at 18F, No. 16, Zhongshan North Road, Section 2, Zhongshan District, Taipei — in the heart of Taipei's elegant Zhongshan commercial and cultural district, a neighborhood of tree-lined boulevards, design boutiques, and a thriving creative and technology scene just north of the city center. Taiwan is the world's most critical semiconductor manufacturing hub — TSMC alone produces the majority of the world's most advanced chips — and Red Hat's enterprise Linux and OpenShift platforms underpin significant portions of Taiwan's technology industry and manufacturing automation. The open source ecosystem here is highly mature and deeply engaged.

Near the Zhongshan office, Le Palais at the Palais de Chine Hotel holds three Michelin stars and is widely recognized as Taipei's finest Cantonese restaurant — a magnificent dining room serving dishes of extraordinary refinement using premium ingredients prepared with generations of mastery. For a more distinctly Taiwanese fine dining experience, RAW by Chef André Chiang is one of Asia's most celebrated restaurants, earning consistent rankings in Asia's 50 Best and earning acclaim for its inventive seasonal Taiwanese cuisine expressed with world-class technique. And of course, Din Tai Fung's original Xinyi Road location — the restaurant that launched a global brand from a single Taipei shop in 1972 — is an essential pilgrimage for any visitor's first evening.

Visiting the Red Hat Taipei office means arriving in one of Asia's most livable, friendly, and underappreciated great cities — a place where an exceptional Metro system, extraordinary street food culture, natural hot springs at Beitou, hiking trails in Yangmingshan National Park, and a thriving creative arts scene all coexist within the city limits. Taiwan's democratic, open, and internationally minded society resonates deeply with Red Hat's values of openness and community, and the local technology community's passion for open source and collaborative development reflects a national culture that understands exactly why open beats closed. 歡迎光臨！
