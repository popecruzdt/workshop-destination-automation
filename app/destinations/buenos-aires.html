Destination Buenos Aires

Buenos Aires, Argentina is home to the Red Hat Argentina office, located at Reconquista 1088, C1003ABV, Buenos Aires — in the Microcentro financial district at the heart of Argentina's capital, steps from the historic Plaza de Mayo on the city's most important financial street. Buenos Aires is one of the great cities of the Southern Hemisphere — the Paris of South America is not merely a nickname but an accurate description of a city of extraordinary architectural grandeur, passionate cultural life, world-class cuisine, and a European sophistication grafted onto South American energy. Argentina has produced exceptional software engineering talent for decades and the Buenos Aires tech community is one of the most creative and technically accomplished in Latin America.

In the Microcentro and across Buenos Aires's celebrated Palermo and San Telmo neighborhoods, Don Julio in Palermo Hollywood is consistently ranked among the world's finest steakhouses — a Porteño institution where the pasture-raised Argentine beef, exceptional Malbec selection from Mendoza, and genuine Buenos Aires warmth combine into an experience that is simply irreplaceable. For Argentina's most celebrated fine dining, Tegui in Palermo earns outstanding acclaim for its sophisticated modern Argentine tasting menu matched with an extraordinary cellar of Argentine wines — the country's terroir expressed at its most thoughtful and ambitious. The Buenos Aires empanada, meanwhile, remains one of humanity's most perfect handheld meals.

Visiting the Red Hat Buenos Aires office means arriving in a city that conducts business with characteristic Argentine passion and elegance — where the lunch meeting extends naturally into three hours, the evening dinner begins at 10pm, the tango milongas run until dawn, and the professional conversations are among the most intellectually invigorating you'll have anywhere in the Red Hat network. The city's neighborhoods — the colorful Caminito in La Boca, the antique markets of San Telmo, the bookshop culture of Palermo — offer a cultural richness that constantly surprises visitors who expected only beef and football. The Argentina team's technical quality and their infectious enthusiasm for open source are among Raleigh HQ's most frequently praised attributes. ¡Dale!
