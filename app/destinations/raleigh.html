Destination Raleigh

Raleigh, North Carolina, USA is home to the global headquarters of Red Hat, located at 100 E. Davie Street, Raleigh, NC 27601 — in the landmark Red Hat Tower in the heart of downtown Raleigh. Red Hat chose Raleigh as its permanent home in 2018 when it opened this iconic building, cementing the city's identity as one of the South's premier technology capitals. The Research Triangle region — connecting Raleigh, Durham, and Chapel Hill — is one of the most concentrated technology and research ecosystems in the United States, home to IBM, Cisco, SAS, and world-class universities including NC State, Duke, and UNC-Chapel Hill.

Steps from Red Hat HQ in downtown Raleigh, Bida Manda is one of the city's most celebrated and beloved restaurants, consistently earning top marks for its inventive Laotian cuisine — a joyful, fragrant menu rooted in the cooking of Chef Vansana Nolintha's childhood. The crispy rice salad, beef laap, and house-made noodle dishes are exceptional, and the restaurant's warm atmosphere has made it a genuine Raleigh institution beloved by the local tech community. The adjacent cocktail bar Brewery Bhavana is equally acclaimed and the combination of both is widely considered the finest evening out in downtown Raleigh.

Visiting Red Hat Raleigh HQ means coming to the home of open source enterprise software — the building where Red Hat Enterprise Linux, OpenShift, and Ansible were shaped into the platforms powering global cloud infrastructure. The Red Hat Amphitheater outside the building hosts live music events all year, the Raleigh City Farm is a blocks away, and the surrounding Research Triangle offers an extraordinary concentration of intellectual talent paired with legendary Southern hospitality. Raleigh is also home to some of North Carolina's finest barbecue, a tradition taken very seriously and one that any visitor should experience before any major technical discussion. Welcome to the Triangle!
