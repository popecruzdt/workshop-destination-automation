Destination Manila

Manila (Makati City), Philippines is home to the Red Hat Philippines office, located at 14F, Ecoplaza Building, 2305 Chino Roces Avenue Extension, Makati City — in the heart of Makati, the Philippines' premier financial and business district and one of Southeast Asia's most important commercial centers. Makati's Bonifacio Global City (BGC) and central business district form the core of the Philippines' rapidly growing technology and business process outsourcing sector — a country of over 110 million people with exceptional English proficiency, deep IT services expertise, and a young, digitally native workforce that is embracing cloud-native technologies with remarkable speed.

In Makati and the broader Metro Manila area, Gallery by Chele in BGC is consistently rated one of the Philippines' finest restaurants, earning top national and regional acclaim for Chef Chele González's extraordinary seasonal tasting menu celebrating Philippine ingredients — rare indigenous produce, exceptional local seafood, and tropical flavors expressed with European technique and genuine philosophical commitment to Filipino culinary heritage. For something beloved by Manila's entire business community, Establishment in BGC and the extraordinary Filipino family-style dining at Manam (a modern celebration of classic Filipino comfort food) are essential experiences that no visitor should miss.

Visiting the Red Hat Manila office means arriving in a city of extraordinary warmth and energy — the Philippines is consistently ranked among the world's most welcoming countries, and Filipino hospitality is not a marketing claim but a genuine cultural value. Manila's food scene is undergoing a remarkable renaissance, its tech sector is growing rapidly, and the nearby island and beach destinations accessible by short domestic flights — Palawan (consistently ranked the world's most beautiful island), Boracay, Siargao — make a Manila business trip uniquely easy to extend into something extraordinary. The Filipino team brings warmth, creativity, and an unshakeable positive outlook to everything they do. Mabuhay!
