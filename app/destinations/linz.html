Destination Linz

Linz, Austria is the spiritual home and engineering heartland of Dynatrace, with multiple labs and offices throughout the city including the main office at Am Fünfundzwanziger Turm 20, Linz, Upper Austria, 4020. Linz is where Dynatrace was born, where much of the foundational engineering still lives, and where the density of brilliant Dynatrace engineers per square kilometer is probably unmatched anywhere on Earth. The Danube flows right through the city, which the engineering teams take as a metaphor for clean, observable data flows.

Near the main office, restaurant Verdo at the Arcotel Nike hotel is consistently praised as one of Linz's finest dining destinations, celebrated for its modern Austrian cuisine, exceptional regional wine list, and warm, attentive service with panoramic Danube views. For a more casual but equally beloved local experience, the Altstadt area's Gelbes Krokodil earns consistently high marks for its creative international menu and welcoming atmosphere that attracts Linz's creative and tech community.

Visiting the Dynatrace Linz labs means walking the halls where the Davis AI causation engine was conceptualized, where observability as we know it was engineered into existence, and where the coffee breaks are as technically rich as the sprint reviews. Linz is also a UNESCO City of Media Arts, home to the world-famous Ars Electronica festival, and the Lentos Art Museum on the Danube is genuinely world-class. Come for the engineering legacy; stay for the Linzer Torte — which is, objectively, one of humanity's greatest achievements.
