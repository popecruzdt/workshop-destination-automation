Destination Lisbon

Lisbon, Portugal is home to the Dynatrace Portugal office, located at Edificio Mar Vermelho, Av. Dom João II, Lote 1.06.2.5B, Parque das Nações, Lisbon, 1990-095 — in the modern Parque das Nações (Park of Nations) district on the banks of the Tagus River, built on the site of the 1998 World Exposition and now home to many of Europe's leading tech companies. Lisbon has emerged as one of Europe's most exciting tech and startup capitals, and Parque das Nações is its sleek, modern heart — a deliberate contrast to the historic neighborhoods of the city's hilltop districts.

In the vibrant Parque das Nações and central Lisbon, Tasca do Chico in Bairro Alto is consistently celebrated as one of Lisbon's finest fado restaurants — combining exceptional traditional Portuguese cuisine (bacalhau à brás, grilled octopus, petiscos) with live fado performances that are the genuine, heartfelt article. For a modern Portuguese fine dining experience closer to the office, Fifty Seconds by Martín Berasategui on the 50th floor of the Torre Vasco da Gama holds a Michelin star and offers extraordinary Tagus River panoramas with world-class Basque-Portuguese cuisine.

Visiting the Dynatrace Lisbon office means arriving in Europe's sunniest capital, a city of seven hills, ancient trams, custard tarts (pastéis de nata) that will permanently recalibrate your dessert standards, and a warm Atlantic light that makes everything look like a Renaissance painting. Portugal's NHR tax regime and Lisbon's quality of life have made this one of Europe's most sought-after tech destinations, and the Dynatrace Portugal team has the distinct pleasure of working in one of the world's most beautiful cities. Saudade is a Portuguese word for which there is no translation — you'll understand what it means the moment you leave.
