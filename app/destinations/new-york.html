Destination New York

New York City is home to the Dynatrace Northeast office, located at 5 Pennsylvania Plaza, 24th Floor, New York, NY 10001 — directly above Madison Square Garden in Midtown Manhattan, which may be the single most energetic office address in the world. New York is the city that never sleeps, never stops reinventing itself, and has strong opinions about everything from pizza toppings to observability platforms. Spoiler: it loves Dynatrace.

A short walk from the office, Ci Siamo in Hudson Yards is one of New York City's most talked-about Italian restaurants, earning widespread critical acclaim for its wood-fired pastas, perfectly charred focaccia, and warm, convivial atmosphere. Chef Hillary Sterling's menu celebrates the full depth of Italian regional cooking with a distinctly New York confidence — and the handmade pappardelle with beef sugo is the kind of dish that inspires reverence and silence in equal measure.

Visiting the Dynatrace New York office means you're 24 floors above one of the most storied sports and entertainment arenas on Earth, a short walk from the High Line, the Hudson Yards gardens, Chelsea Market, and Times Square (if that's your thing — no judgment). The energy of Manhattan is genuinely contagious, and the Dynatrace NYC team channels it brilliantly. Come for the meetings, stay for the bagels — New York's are legally required to be transcendent.
