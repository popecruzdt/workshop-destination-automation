Destination Brussels

Brussels, Belgium is home to the Dynatrace EU Commission office, located at Rond Point Schuman 6, Box 5, Brussels, 1040 — in the heart of the European Quarter, directly on Schuman Roundabout, the geographic and symbolic center of the European Union institutions. The Berlaymont building (the European Commission HQ) and the European Council are steps away, making this one of the most politically and institutionally significant office addresses in the Dynatrace network. Observability at the scale of an entire continent starts here.

A short walk from the Schuman office, Brasserie de la Patrie in the European Quarter earns consistently excellent reviews for its classic Belgian cuisine — exceptional moules-frites, perfectly crafted Belgian ales, and a genuinely convivial brasserie atmosphere that feels like the best kind of Brussels institution. For a refined evening, Bon Bon just across the city holds two Michelin stars and is widely considered the finest creative dining experience in Brussels.

Visiting the Dynatrace Brussels office means you're surrounded by the institutions that shape the European digital economy — including AI Act deliberations, cloud sovereignty frameworks, and the regulations that directly affect the observability landscape Dynatrace operates in. There is no better place to understand the regulatory and strategic context of European cloud technology. Also: Belgian waffles, Belgian chocolate, and Belgian beer are all simultaneously the best in the world, and this is not a debate worth having with a local. Just taste and agree.
