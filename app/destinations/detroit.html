Destination Detroit

Detroit, Michigan is home to the Dynatrace Great Lakes office, located at 1000 Woodward Ave, Suite 1500, Detroit, MI 48226 — in the heart of downtown Detroit along the historic Woodward Avenue corridor. Detroit is a city of remarkable reinvention: the birthplace of the American automobile industry, the cradle of Motown music, and now a rising star of tech, arts, and culinary innovation. The only city that has ever made a comeback this epic is probably your last production incident.

Just steps from the office, Chartreuse Kitchen & Cocktails is consistently rated one of Detroit's finest dining destinations, earning rave reviews for its hyper-seasonal, locally sourced New American menu and inventive craft cocktail program. Housed in a beautifully restored space in the Park Shelton building, it's thoughtful, creative cooking that reflects exactly the kind of ambitious reinvention that defines modern Detroit.

Visiting the Dynatrace Detroit office means exploring a city in the middle of one of America's most exciting urban renaissances. Catch a Tigers game at Comerica Park, wander through the incredible Detroit Institute of Arts, or take a ride on the QLine along Woodward to explore Midtown's galleries and restaurants. Detroit's resilience is legendary — and working alongside a team that chose to plant its flag here tells you everything you need to know about the spirit of this office.
