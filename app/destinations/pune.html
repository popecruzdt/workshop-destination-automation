Destination Pune

Pune, Maharashtra, India is home to a major Red Hat India engineering office, located at Tower 4, Panchshil Business Park, Yerwada, Pune 411 006 — in the prestigious Panchshil Business Park in Yerwada, one of Pune's most established corporate technology zones. Pune is India's third-largest technology hub, a city of universities, engineering colleges, and automotive and IT industries, often called the Oxford of the East for its exceptional concentration of educational institutions. Red Hat's Pune engineering hub is one of the most important development centers in the company's global network, with deep contributions to upstream OpenShift, Ansible, and RHEL platform development.

In the Yerwada and central Pune area, Malaka Spice in Koregaon Park is one of Pune's most celebrated restaurants, consistently earning top marks for its outstanding Southeast Asian cuisine — particularly its Thai, Vietnamese, and Indonesian dishes — in a lush garden setting that makes it the undisputed favorite of Pune's IT professional community for weekend lunches and client dinners. For exceptional local Maharashtrian cuisine, Sinhagad Fort — a short but dramatic drive from the office — offers the rare experience of authentic Puneri thali at the food stalls near the historical fort, with views across the Western Ghats that are among the finest in Maharashtra.

Visiting the Red Hat Pune office means arriving in a city that takes engineering seriously — Pune's Deccan Gymkhana area, Koregaon Park's tree-lined lanes, and the ancient Aga Khan Palace all exist within walking distance of an IT sector producing some of India's most talented platform engineers. The monsoon season transforms the surrounding Sahyadri mountains into a waterfall-filled landscape of breathtaking beauty, and the Osho Meditation Resort in Koregaon Park is one of the world's most visited meditation centers for those who need to decompress after particularly intense OpenShift sprint planning sessions. Pune is focused, educated, and deeply productive — essential Red Hat qualities all.
