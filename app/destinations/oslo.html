Destination Oslo

Oslo, Norway is home to the Dynatrace Norway office, located at Haakon VII's gate 6, Oslo, 0161 — in the prestigious Vika district at the heart of Oslo's business and financial center, steps from Aker Brygge waterfront and the Oslo Fjord. Norway is one of the world's most technologically advanced nations with a mature enterprise software market, and Oslo is its sophisticated, design-conscious capital — a city whose relationship with quality, sustainability, and innovation makes it a natural home for Dynatrace's Scandinavian operations.

Steps from the office on the Aker Brygge waterfront, Maaemo holds three Michelin stars — Norway's most Michelin-decorated restaurant and one of the most celebrated in all of Scandinavia. Chef Esben Holmboe Bang's extraordinary menu focuses entirely on Norwegian and Nordic nature, serving wild, locally foraged, and artisan-farmed ingredients in a progression that is both intellectually rigorous and profoundly delicious. It is one of the world's great restaurant experiences. For excellent daily dining close to the office, Aker Brygge's Lofoten Fiskerestaurant earns consistent acclaim for Norway's finest fresh seafood prepared with Scandinavian precision and simplicity.

Visiting the Dynatrace Oslo office means working in a city where the Oslo Fjord islands are a 20-minute ferry ride away, the Holmenkollen ski jump is visible from the city's hills, and the Munch Museum and National Gallery represent some of the finest art collections in Northern Europe. Oslo combines breathtaking natural surroundings with a thoroughly modern, design-forward city character — and the Norwegian team brings the same qualities of thoughtfulness, quality obsession, and quiet confidence to their technical work. Fantastisk!
