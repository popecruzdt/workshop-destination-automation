Destination Dublin

Dublin, Ireland is home to the Dynatrace Ireland office, located at Regus House, Harcourt Centre, Block 4, Harcourt Road, Dublin 2, D02 HW77 — in the heart of Dublin's Grand Canal District, the city's premier corporate and tech quarter. Dublin is the European home of some of the world's most influential technology companies, and a city whose combination of English-language business environment, exceptional engineering talent, and genuine warmth of character make it one of the most welcoming places in the Dynatrace network.

A short walk from the office, Restaurant Patrick Guilbaud on Merrion Street holds two Michelin stars and is Ireland's most acclaimed fine dining restaurant, celebrated for its exquisite French-Irish cuisine, impeccable service, and a wine list of extraordinary depth. For something more relaxed but equally local and excellent, The Pig's Ear on Nassau Street earns consistent top marks for its creative, playful take on classic Irish ingredients elevated to genuinely sophisticated cuisine — a perfect reflection of modern Dublin's culinary ambition.

Visiting the Dynatrace Dublin office means arriving in one of Europe's most genuinely friendly cities, where the pub culture is a legitimate social infrastructure, the craic is always mighty, and the literary heritage of Joyce, Beckett, and Wilde seems to permeate every cobblestone lane in the Georgian city center. The Irish team is known for its warmth, its technical depth, and its ability to debate cloud architecture and Gaelic football with equal passion. Whether it's your first pint of Guinness or your hundredth, Dublin has a way of making you feel like you've come home.
