Destination Barcelona

Barcelona, Spain is home to Dynatrace Lab Barcelona, located at Avinguda Diagonal, 211, Barcelona, 08018 — on the iconic Diagonal boulevard in the Poblenou district, Barcelona's emerging tech and innovation corridor known as the 22@ Innovation District. Poblenou was once Barcelona's industrial heartland and is rapidly transforming into one of Europe's most dynamic tech campuses, with a unique blend of converted factories, modern office buildings, and some of the most sought-after tech talent in Southern Europe. The Mediterranean is a 10-minute walk away.

In the 22@ and city center area, Disfrutar earns three Michelin stars and is consistently ranked among the world's top five restaurants — its three-chef team (all alumni of elBulli) creates one of the most technically extraordinary and joyful tasting menu experiences in the world. For an exceptional but more accessible experience near the office, Roca Moo in the Eixample district holds one Michelin star and earns top marks for its modern Catalan cuisine and outstanding natural wine selection focusing on small producers.

Visiting the Dynatrace Barcelona lab means working in a city where the Sagrada Família is still being built (since 1882!), the beach is reachable by bike from the office, and the local food markets like Mercat de Sant Antoni set a global benchmark for what a neighborhood market should be. The Barcelona team brings Catalan creativity, precision, and a healthy dose of Mediterranean optimism to everything it builds — and the post-work tapas at a local bodega are a non-negotiable part of the engineering process.
