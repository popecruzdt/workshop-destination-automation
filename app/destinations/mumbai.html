Destination Mumbai

Mumbai, Maharashtra, India is home to one of the Dynatrace India offices, located at 20th Floor, Parinee Crescenzo, Kautilya Bhawan-2, BKC, G Block BKC, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra East, Mumbai, Maharashtra, 400051 — in the Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai's premier financial and corporate district and home to India's major banks, multinational headquarters, and financial regulators. Mumbai is India's commercial and financial capital, a city of extraordinary energy and contrasts, and the beating heart of the Indian economy that Dynatrace helps power through its enterprise clients.

The BKC area and greater Mumbai offer world-class dining at every turn. Masque in Mahalaxmi is consistently ranked India's finest restaurant, earning national and international critical acclaim for its extraordinary ingredient-led tasting menu rooted in India's vast regional food traditions — foraging, fermenting, and sourcing from across the subcontinent to create a deeply personal and technically exceptional dining experience. For a beautiful BKC-area option, The Table at Colaba (reachable easily) or the celebrated Kode in BKC itself are outstanding choices that reliably earn top marks from Mumbai's business community.

Visiting the Dynatrace Mumbai office means arriving in a city that never, ever stops. The local trains carry over 7 million passengers a day, the city produces more films than Hollywood, the street food scene at Mohammed Ali Road and Juhu Beach are legendary, and the Gateway of India at Apollo Bunder is one of the great urban waterfront experiences in Asia. The Mumbai team brings the city's characteristic drive, resilience, and commercial intelligence to everything it does — and a chai break here is never just a chai break, it's a conversation that moves quickly and goes somewhere interesting.
