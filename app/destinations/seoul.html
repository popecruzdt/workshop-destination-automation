Destination Seoul

Seoul, South Korea is home to Dynatrace Korea Ltd., located at #09-122, 9F, 7, Teheran-ro 5-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul 06134 — just off the famous Teheran-ro corridor in Gangnam, Korea's premier tech and business district and the heart of the Korean Silicon Valley. Gangnam is where Samsung, LG, Hyundai, and dozens of Korea's most important technology companies have their operations, and Teheran-ro is lined with the offices of Korea's most dynamic startups and global tech company representatives. Seoul is one of the world's most digitally advanced cities, with the fastest average internet speeds on Earth and a consumer technology culture that sets global trends.

In the Gangnam district, La Yeon at the The Shilla Seoul hotel holds three Michelin stars and is South Korea's most acclaimed Korean fine dining restaurant — presenting classical Korean royal cuisine (Joseon Dynasty court food) with extraordinary refinement, seasonal precision, and a depth of cultural storytelling that makes every course both a culinary and historical experience. For an equally exceptional but more casual Seoul experience, the grilled meat restaurants (gopchang jeongol and galbi) of Gangnam's Nonhyeon-dong neighborhood are among the world's great meat-eating experiences, approached with the seriousness they deserve.

Visiting the Dynatrace Seoul office means arriving in a city that appears in global pop culture (K-Pop, K-Drama, Korean cinema), produces some of the world's most advanced consumer electronics and semiconductors, and whose residents are among the most digitally connected people on Earth — making them among the world's most sophisticated appreciators of what Dynatrace observability can do. The Han River parks, Gyeongbokgung Palace, the Bukchon Hanok Village, and the shopping and entertainment centers of Myeongdong offer a city experience of remarkable range. The energy here is contagious and the technical conversations are always fast-moving. Annyeong!
