Destination Melbourne

Melbourne, Victoria, Australia is home to the Dynatrace Australia Victoria office, located at 477 Collins Street, Melbourne, VIC 3000 — on the prestigious Collins Street "Paris end" of Melbourne's CBD, in the heart of one of Australia's most culturally sophisticated and livable cities. Melbourne consistently ranks among the world's most livable cities and has Australia's most enthusiastic coffee culture, a world-class arts and dining scene, and a tech sector that spans finance, retail, health, and logistics. The Dynatrace Melbourne office is central to the company's Australian operations.

Steps from 477 Collins Street, Vue de Monde on level 55 of the Rialto Towers is Melbourne's most celebrated fine dining destination — earning consistent national and international acclaim for its inventive, theatrically presented modern Australian tasting menu created from foraged and farmed ingredients across Victoria and beyond. For Melbourne's legendary café culture (which the city takes every bit as seriously as observability), Proud Mary on Smith Street is considered a global benchmark for specialty coffee, with a procession of industry professionals making pilgrimages to its roastery counter.

Visiting the Dynatrace Melbourne office means arriving in Australia's undisputed cultural capital — a city of laneway bars, street art, AFL football religion, the world's most sophisticated brunch culture, and institutions like the Melbourne Symphony Orchestra and Australian Open that set global standards. Melbourne locals have a good-natured rivalry with Sydney (the answer is: Melbourne wins on food, coffee, and liveability; Sydney wins on harbour views and weather; everyone is happy), and the Melbourne Dynatrace team channels the city's creative, curious, quality-obsessed energy into everything they build.
