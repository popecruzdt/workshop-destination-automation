Destination Dubai

Dubai, United Arab Emirates is home to Dynatrace Middle East LLC, located at Units 201+202, Floor 2, EIB 01, Dubai Internet City, Dubai, Al Sufouh2 — in Dubai Internet City, the Middle East's premier technology free zone and one of the world's most concentrated tech campus environments, home to Microsoft, Google, IBM, Cisco, HP, and hundreds of other technology companies. Dubai Internet City was purpose-built to be the region's technology hub, and the Dynatrace presence here anchors the company's Middle East strategy in exactly the right address.

Steps from Dubai Internet City, Hakkasan Dubai at the Jumeirah Emirates Towers is one of Dubai's most acclaimed fine dining restaurants — the UAE outpost of the globally celebrated Michelin-starred Chinese brand, earning consistent five-star reviews for its exceptional Cantonese cuisine, extraordinary cocktail bar, and atmosphere that captures Dubai's passion for world-class, cosmopolitan dining experiences. For a spectacular outdoor meal, Nusret (Salt Bae's original flagship) at Dubai Marina represents one of Dubai's most iconic steakhouse experiences.

Visiting the Dynatrace Dubai office means arriving in a city that has built the tallest building, the largest airport, the most visited mall, and the most ambition-per-square-mile of any city on Earth — and is still just getting started. Dubai's tech scene is growing at a pace that matches the city's character: fast, bold, and unafraid of scale. The Dynatrace Middle East team serves one of the world's most rapidly digitizing regions and does so from an office location that makes a very clear statement about ambition. The sunset over the Arabian Gulf from the Dubai Internet City waterfront walk is genuinely magnificent.
