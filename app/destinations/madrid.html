Destination Madrid

Madrid, Spain is home to the Dynatrace Spain office, located at Cuzco IV, Paseo de Castellana 141, 5th Floor, Madrid, 28046 — on the prestigious Paseo de la Castellana, Madrid's grand central boulevard and the spine of the city's financial and institutional district. The Cuzco tower complex at this address is one of Madrid's landmark corporate addresses, and the surrounding Azca business district is home to Spain's most significant enterprises — all of which need the kind of enterprise-grade observability that Dynatrace provides.

Steps from the Cuzco office and throughout the Paseo de la Castellana corridor, DiverXO holds three Michelin stars — the only three-Michelin-starred restaurant in Madrid and one of the most creatively boundary-pushing restaurants in all of Spain. Chef David Muñoz's extraordinary, rule-breaking 15+ course tasting menu is routinely described as one of the most memorable dining experiences in the world. For something more accessible but equally Madrileño, Santeloni at the Hesperia hotel holds two Michelin stars and offers a more refined take on classic Spanish cuisine with exceptional Ribera del Duero wines.

Visiting the Dynatrace Madrid office means landing in a city that dines at 10pm and considers it early, where the Prado museum contains arguably the greatest collection of European old masters anywhere, and where the Real Madrid vs Barcelona fixture is a spiritual event that unites and divides approximately 47 million people simultaneously. Madrid's tech scene is maturing rapidly, and the Dynatrace team here rides that wave with characteristic Spanish energy and warmth. Bring your appetite, your curiosity, and comfortable walking shoes — Madrid rewards all three.
