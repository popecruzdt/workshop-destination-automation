Destination Bangkok

Bangkok, Thailand is home to the Red Hat Thailand office, located at Uma Tower, Floors 22–23, 968 Rama IV Road, Bangkok 10500 — in the Silom/Sathorn district, Bangkok's premier financial and international business corridor, home to the offices of virtually every major multinational operating in Southeast Asia. Bangkok is the gateway city to mainland Southeast Asia and one of the world's most visited cities — a place of extraordinary contrasts between ancient temples, modern skyscrapers, legendary street food, and five-star hospitality. Thailand's digital economy is growing rapidly and Red Hat supports this transformation through enterprise Linux, automation, and OpenShift engagements across the region.

Steps from the Uma Tower office in the Silom district, Nahm at the COMO Metropolitan Hotel is one of Southeast Asia's most acclaimed restaurants, earning consistent top rankings in Asia's 50 Best for its extraordinary authentic Thai royal cuisine — Chef Pim Techamuanvivit's team prepares dishes of remarkable complexity and historicity from ancient recipes, using the freshest local ingredients in a dining experience that represents Thai culinary tradition at its most refined and ambitious. For the genuine Bangkok street food experience — which is an essential part of any visit to the city — the Silom Soi 20 night market and the nearby Chinatown's Yaowarat Road are internationally celebrated as among the world's finest.

Visiting the Red Hat Bangkok office means arriving in one of the world's great cities for the sheer adventure of existing within it — a city where the Grand Palace and Wat Pho are a 20-minute BTS Skytrain ride from a Michelin-starred restaurant, where the night markets, rooftop bars, and Chao Phraya river boat taxis coexist entirely naturally, and where Thai hospitality (sabai sabai — the philosophy of relaxed, gracious ease) infuses every professional interaction. Bangkok's tech community is growing, international, and very enthusiastic about open source automation — Ansible is particularly well loved in a region that understands deeply the value of doing complex things elegantly.
