Destination Brno

Brno, Czech Republic is home to Dynatrace Brno, located at Building H, Přízova 9, 602 00 Brno-střed, Czechia — in the dynamic city center of the Czech Republic's second-largest city. Brno is a vibrant university city with a thriving tech scene, a remarkably affordable quality of life, and a strong cultural identity built around architecture, design, and innovation. The Dynatrace Brno office draws from one of Central Europe's richest pools of software engineering and data science talent.

Near the Dynatrace office in central Brno, Pavillon is consistently ranked among the city's top fine dining experiences, earning praise for its modern European cuisine, elegant atmosphere, and attentive service that reflects Brno's growing confidence as a Czech culinary destination. For a more casual but equally beloved experience, Spolek is a favorite among Brno's tech community for its creative Czech-international menu, excellent natural wines, and a laid-back atmosphere that's perfect for a post-sprint debrief.

Visiting the Dynatrace Brno office means exploring a city where the famous Functionalist architecture of Villa Tugendhat (a UNESCO World Heritage Site) sits alongside buzzing creative hubs and one of Central Europe's most energetic nightlife and cultural scenes. Brno locals are known for their dry wit, technical precision, and an almost stubborn insistence on doing things properly — qualities that align extremely well with building world-class observability software. The Janáček Theatre and Špilberk Castle are both unmissable while you're there.
