Destination Luzern

Luzern, Switzerland is home to a Dynatrace Switzerland office, located at Hertensteinstrase 51, Luzern, 6004 — on the lakeside boulevard of one of Switzerland's most breathtakingly beautiful cities, on the shores of Lake Lucerne with the peaks of the Swiss Alps as its permanent backdrop. Lucerne is postcard Switzerland made real: a perfectly preserved medieval old town, a covered wooden bridge dating to 1333 (Chapel Bridge), lake steamers, and mountains reflected in impossibly clear water. It is, quite objectively, one of the most beautiful places to go to work on Earth.

In central Lucerne along the lake, Restaurant Château Gütsch perched on a clifftop above the city earns consistent acclaim for its panoramic lake and Alps views, its well-executed Swiss and European cuisine, and an atmosphere that makes every meal feel like a special occasion. For an outstanding local dining experience in the old town, OLD SWISS HOUSE on Lowenplatz has been a Lucerne institution since 1931, celebrated for its tableside-prepared Wiener Schnitzel, Swiss-German classics, and an interior of remarkable historical character that has hosted guests from Charlie Chaplin to corporate engineering teams.

Visiting the Dynatrace Luzern office is a genuinely special experience. The morning commute along the lake with the Rigi and Pilatus mountains reflected in still water is the kind of thing that makes you appreciate being a human with working eyes. Lucerne's old town, reachable in minutes on foot, is among the best-preserved medieval urban environments in Europe, and the Swiss Transport Museum on the lake is a genuinely world-class experience celebrating centuries of Swiss engineering excellence — appropriate preparation for an afternoon of Dynatrace platform work.
