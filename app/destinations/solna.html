Destination Solna

Solna, Sweden is home to the Dynatrace Sweden office, located at Rosenborgsgatan 12, Solna, 169 74 — in the Frösunda business district of Solna, directly adjacent to Stockholm and minutes from the Swedish capital's city center by commuter rail or bus. Solna is home to some of Sweden's most important enterprises and institutions including Karolinska Institut, and the Dynatrace Sweden office is ideally positioned to serve the Nordic market from a location that combines the dynamic business culture of greater Stockholm with a slightly calmer character than the city center.

A short commute from the office in central Stockholm, Frantzén holds three Michelin stars and is widely considered the finest restaurant in Scandinavia — Chef Björn Frantzén's 20+ course tasting menu seamlessly blends Japanese precision with Nordic ingredients and French technique in one of the world's most sought-after dining experiences. For something brilliant and more accessible, Operakällaren in the Royal Opera House on Kungsträdgården is a Stockholm institution that has been serving exceptional Swedish and European cuisine for over 130 years, with a level of elegance that matches its extraordinary address.

Visiting the Dynatrace Solna/Stockholm office means arriving in a city that invents things: the adjustable wrench, the zipper, the pacemaker, Spotify, Skype, Minecraft, and King (Candy Crush) all come from Sweden — which gives you some sense of why the local engineering culture is so deeply confident and prolific. The Stockholm Archipelago's 30,000 islands, the ABBA Museum, and the midnight sun in June are all compelling reasons to extend a business trip into something unforgettable. Skål!
