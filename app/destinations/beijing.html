Destination Beijing

Beijing, China is home to the Dynatrace China North office, located at Office 345, 3F, Vantone Centre Tower D, No. 6 Chaoyangmenwai Avenue, Chaoyang District, Beijing, 100020 — in the heart of Beijing's Chaoyang central business district, one of China's most important commercial and diplomatic zones. Beijing is the capital of the world's second-largest economy, a city of extraordinary historical depth and dynamic modern ambition, home to enterprises of global scale, and a rapidly growing technology sector where cloud-native transformation is proceeding at remarkable speed.

A short walk from the Vantone Centre in Chaoyang, Da Dong Roast Duck Restaurant on Dongsishitiao is one of Beijing's most celebrated and internationally recognized restaurants — established in 1985 and now a Beijing institution, earning widespread acclaim for its crispy, low-fat Peking Duck prepared using an innovative oven technique, served with the complete traditional accompaniments. The dish is one of the world's great culinary experiences and eating it at Da Dong in Beijing, where it was invented, is an experience that simply cannot be replicated elsewhere.

Visiting the Dynatrace Beijing office means standing at the intersection of 3,000 years of continuous civilization and one of the fastest-moving digital economies on Earth. The Forbidden City, the Great Wall at Mutianyu, the 798 Art District, and the hutong neighborhoods of old Beijing are all within reach, offering an experience of extraordinary cultural depth and contrast. The Beijing team navigates a technology landscape of remarkable complexity and scale with impressive expertise — and a shared meal of Peking Duck after a productive day is as close to a universal business ritual as exists anywhere in the Dynatrace network.
