Destination Boulogne-Billancourt

Boulogne-Billancourt, France is home to the Dynatrace France office, located at Batiment ILEO, 27/33 Quai Alphonse Le Gallo, Boulogne-Billancourt, 92100 — along the banks of the Seine in the premier tech and media suburb just southwest of Paris. Boulogne-Billancourt is where some of France's most influential tech and media companies are headquartered, and the ILEO building is a contemporary landmark in this vibrant riverside locale. The Paris city center is a direct Metro Line 9 ride away from the office.

Steps from the office along the Seine, La Gueuze is a beloved local brasserie celebrated for its exceptional Belgian and French craft beer selection, outstanding steak-frites, and the relaxed, convivial atmosphere that embodies the best of French brasserie culture. For a more refined evening, Le Duc in nearby Paris earns two Michelin stars and consistent top-tier reviews for its impeccable fish and seafood cuisine — a Parisian institution of the highest order.

Visiting the Dynatrace Boulogne-Billancourt office means you're perfectly positioned to experience both the cutting-edge energy of France's tech corridor and the full cultural splendor of Paris — the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, and the Seine riverbanks are all minutes away. The French team brings the country's characteristic blend of intellectual rigor and joie de vivre to everything they do, and the office lunch conversations will simultaneously cover Kubernetes observability and the correct method for achieving a perfect beurre blanc. Magnifique!
