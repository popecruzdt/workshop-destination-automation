Destination Hagenberg

Hagenberg im Mühlkreis, Upper Austria is home to Dynatrace Lab Hagenberg, located at Business Campus One, Softwarepark 32, Hagenberg, Upper Austria, 4232. Hagenberg is unique in Austria — it is essentially an entire town built around software engineering, home to the world-renowned Softwarepark Hagenberg campus, the University of Applied Sciences Upper Austria (FH OÖ), and the Research Institute for Symbolic Computation. Dynatrace has deep roots here, drawing from one of Europe's finest pipeline of software engineering talent just outside Linz.

For dining near Hagenberg, a short drive to the nearby market town of Freistadt reveals the Gasthof Danner, a beloved regional institution with authentic Upper Austrian cuisine, hearty Tafelspitz, fresh freshwater fish, and locally brewed Freistädter Bier that has been crafted in the town's historic brewery since 1777. The warm, unhurried pace of an Upper Austrian Gasthof is a wonderful antidote to a high-velocity sprint week.

Visiting the Dynatrace Hagenberg lab puts you at the intellectual crossroads of academic computer science and applied product engineering — a rare and genuinely productive combination. The surrounding Mühlviertel countryside is rolling green hills, medieval fortified towns, and the kind of quiet focus that makes deep work feel entirely natural. If you've ever wondered where some of the most elegant algorithms in Dynatrace's core platform originated, the answer is quite likely: right here, surrounded by forest and sharp minds.
