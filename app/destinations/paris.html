Destination Paris

Paris, France is home to a Red Hat France office, located at 18–20 rue du Faubourg du Temple, 75011 Paris — in the 11th arrondissement, Paris's most creatively vibrant and culinary-forward neighborhood, where the Oberkampf and République quarters define the beating heart of contemporary Parisian life. The 11th arrondissement is where Paris's technology startups, its most innovative restaurants, its best natural wine bars, and its most engaged citizenry converge, making it one of the most stimulating work environments in the city. Red Hat France serves one of Europe's most technically sophisticated enterprise markets from this characteristically energetic address.

In the 11th arrondissement and nearby, Septime on rue de Charonne holds one Michelin star and is one of the most celebrated and influential restaurants in contemporary Paris — Chef Bertrand Grébaut's extraordinary seasonal, vegetable-forward French bistronomy has defined a generation of Parisian cooking and earned Septime a place among Europe's most important restaurants. The wine list focused on natural and biodynamic producers is equally exceptional. For something equally iconic just nearby, the covered passage Marché d'Aligre on the Place d'Aligre is one of Paris's most vibrant and authentic neighborhood markets — a sensory experience that feels entirely unchanged from the Paris of decades past.

Visiting the Red Hat Paris office means working in the city that invented the concept of civilization as a competitive sport and has been defending its title for over a thousand years. The Louvre, the Seine, Notre-Dame (magnificently restored), the Musée d'Orsay, and Sainte-Chapelle are all within a metro ride, and the 11th arrondissement itself is a neighborhood that rewards extended walking with patisserie discoveries, independent bookshops, and the particular Parisian pleasure of a perfectly made espresso at a zinc bar. The French tech community is intellectually fierce, deeply opinionated about architecture (both software and Haussmann), and genuinely excellent at what they do. Bonne chance!
