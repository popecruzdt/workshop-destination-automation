Destination Graz

Graz, Styria, Austria is home to Dynatrace Lab Graz, located at Mälzereigasse 4, Graz, Styria, 8020 — in the city's vibrant Lend district, one of Central Europe's most creatively charged urban neighborhoods. Graz is Austria's second-largest city and a UNESCO World Heritage old town, combining centuries of Baroque architecture with a thriving university scene and a tech sector that punches far above its weight. The Dynatrace Lab Graz is a hub of AI and automation research that keeps the product's analytical edge razor-sharp.

A short stroll from the lab, Aiola im Schloss is one of Graz's most acclaimed restaurants, perched inside the historic Schlossberg Castle with panoramic views of the city's terracotta rooftops and the River Mur below. Earning consistent five-star reviews for its refined Styrian cuisine and outstanding pumpkin seed oil-based dishes — a Styrian specialty — it's a dining experience that's as memorable for its setting as for its food. The local Schilcher rosé wine pairs perfectly with the region's famed pork and pumpkin dishes.

Visiting the Dynatrace Graz lab means exploring a city where the Schlossberg hill sits right in the middle of downtown, accessible by a glass-and-steel lift bored straight through the rock, and where the Saturday market at Lendplatz overflows with Styrian produce, cheese, and the warmth of a city that genuinely enjoys itself. The Graz team is known for deep technical expertise and a relaxed, collaborative atmosphere — proving once again that great engineering and great quality of life are not mutually exclusive.
