Destination Zurich

Zurich, Switzerland is home to the Dynatrace Switzerland office, located at Uraniastrasse 31, Zurich, 8001 — in the heart of Zurich's city center, steps from the main Hauptbahnhof train station and the Bahnhofstrasse, one of the world's most prestigious shopping streets. Zurich is Switzerland's largest city and financial capital, consistently ranking among the world's top cities for quality of life, and a hub for global finance, pharmaceuticals, and an increasingly influential tech sector. The precision, quality, and reliability that define Swiss culture are entirely consistent with what Dynatrace builds.

Steps from the Dynatrace office in central Zurich, Kronenhalle on Rämistrasse is one of Switzerland's most iconic and beloved restaurants — a Zurich institution since 1924, decorated with original Picasso, Matisse, and Miró artworks, serving exceptional Swiss and European brasserie classics with a level of consistent excellence that has made it the dining room of choice for artists, bankers, and engineers for a century. The Wiener Schnitzel and the house trolley desserts are legendary. The Zurich Geschnetzeltes (sliced veal in cream sauce with rösti) is mandatory for first-time visitors.

Visiting the Dynatrace Zurich office means working in a city where the trains run to the second, the lake is clean enough to swim in through the summer, and the Alps are literally visible on a clear day from the city center. Zurich's tech scene — with Google, IBM, Disney Research, and ETH Zurich all in the city — creates an extraordinary concentration of engineering talent that the Dynatrace Switzerland team is very much part of. The Swiss work philosophy of "if it's worth doing, it's worth doing right, quietly, and on schedule" is something the global Dynatrace organization deeply appreciates.
