Destination Hong Kong

Hong Kong is home to Dynatrace Asia Pacific Ltd., located at 66/F, The Center, 99 Queen's Road, Central, Hong Kong — on one of the highest floors of one of the most striking skyscrapers in an already extraordinary skyline. The Center was designed by Cesar Pelli with a spectacular LED facade and its 66th floor location makes the Dynatrace Hong Kong office one of the highest in the network, with panoramic views of Victoria Harbour, Kowloon, and on clear days, the mountains of southern China. Hong Kong is one of Asia's great financial capitals and a critical hub for Dynatrace's regional strategy.

Close to the Central office, Lung King Heen at the Four Seasons Hotel Hong Kong holds three Michelin stars — the world's first Chinese restaurant to receive that distinction, and still widely considered the finest Cantonese restaurant on Earth. Its dim sum and Cantonese seafood are extraordinary and served with a level of precision, refinement, and hospitality that sets a global standard. For an elevated but more casual experience, Yung Kee on Wellington Street has been a Hong Kong institution for over 75 years, celebrated for roast goose that is, by local consensus, simply the best in the world.

Visiting the Dynatrace Hong Kong office means experiencing one of the world's most kinetic, vertical, and sensory-rich cities — a place where the Star Ferry still connects Kowloon to Central, the night markets of Temple Street pulse with energy, the Peak Tram offers a perspective on urban density unlike anywhere else, and the hiking trails on Lantau Island, minutes from the financial center, pass through genuine wilderness. Hong Kong's character — intense, efficient, deeply curious, internationally minded — is entirely reflected in the team that operates from its extraordinary 66th-floor vantage point.
